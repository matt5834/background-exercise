Add a Background Part Introduction
One of the most challenging hurdles for our young composers is controlling consonance and dissonance when adding their first background part to their main melody. We have leveraged Noteflight's API to build exercises that give learners instant feedback when making note selections for their harmony parts. Planned implemenations include the functionality to give users additional details about the notes they've chosen as well as other ehancements. Please send feedback and comments to support@yciw.net Thank you! YCIW Team
